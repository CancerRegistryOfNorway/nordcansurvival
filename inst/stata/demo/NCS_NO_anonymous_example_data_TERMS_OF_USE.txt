TERMS OF USE:

The anonymous example data [NCS_NO_anonymous_example_data.dta] is provided from Cancer Registry of Norway, who has the ownership to the data, for limited use within the NORDCAN survival package only.

The anonymous example data [NCS_NO_anonymous_example_data.dta] SHOULD NOT be copied, changed, redistributed, or used in any settings other than as provided by the NORDCAN survival package.

The example data are anonymous, which means that the data are rendered anonymous in such a manner that the data subject no longer can be identified. Thus, the processing of these data falls outside the scope of the GDPR.
